How To Play

  1. Enter your bet amount (minimum 10)
  2. Select a color to bet on (Red, Green, Blue, or Violet)
  3. Click "Place Bet" to confirm your bet
  4. Wait for the timer to count down
  5. If the result matches your color, you win multiplied by the payout rate
  6. You can "Cash Out" early to secure your winnings
